Who are we?

We are Benjamin Kahn, Cameron Irving, Edmund Vu, and Tommy Trinh. Just some Chapman Univeristy Comp Sci students.

What is our project?

We are creating a website to rate websites, articles, authors, and more on their credibility.

Why are we doing this?

There is a lot of fake news out there today and we want people to be able to know if what they are reading online is accurate or not.

How does this work?

We have created an algorithm based on several relevant factors in order to create a score for every article on a 0-10 scale.

What can I do to help?

By inputting a website, you can help increase the size of our database to help find more uncredibile news sources.
